Contributions & Refunds

Return Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Saarthi operates as a dedicated non-profit connection platform. Our mission is to facilitate lifesaving blood donations between volunteers and patients. Because we do not sell physical products or commercial services, our return policy is governed by the specialized nature of our work.

Blood Donations

Blood donations are acts of voluntary goodwill and medical necessity. Once a donation procedure has been completed at a medical facility, it cannot be "returned" or "refunded." If you have any concerns regarding the safety, hygiene, or conduct during a donation session, we advise you to contact the hospital or blood bank where the procedure took place immediately.

Monetary Contributions

We deeply appreciate individuals who provide voluntary monetary contributions to help maintain our AI matching technology and server costs. Should you need to request a refund for a contribution made in error, please contact us at support@saarthi.org within 7 calendar days of the transaction.

Non-Commercial Nature

Saarthi does not charge patients for blood or charge donors for their service. Any attempt to solicit funds in exchange for blood on our platform is a violation of our Terms of Service and should be reported immediately.
