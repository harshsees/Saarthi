How Saarthi works

From emergency to lifesaver in four simple steps.

1

Post a Request

Enter blood group, units needed, hospital location and urgency level.

2

AI Matching

Our ML model instantly ranks and identifies the best matching donors nearby.

3

Donors Notified

Matched donors receive the request and can accept or decline with one click.

4

Life Saved

Donor contacts the patient, confirms details, and heads to the hospital.
