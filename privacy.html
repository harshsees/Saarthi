Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

At Saarthi, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to connect with blood donors.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to facilitate the emergency matching process:

  • Account Credentials: Your name, email address, and verified phone number.
  • Medical Details: Your blood group and donation eligibility history.
  • Proximity Data: Your city and real-time geographic coordinates (only collected when you are actively searching for or offering help).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the purpose of matching donors with critical blood requests in real-time. We operate on a strictly non-commercial basis and never sell or lease your personal information to third-party marketers or data brokers.

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard AES-256 encryption to safeguard your sensitive information. Contact details (like phone numbers) are only revealed to a matched donor or patient after both parties have explicitly consented to the connection within the app.

Your Controls

You have full control over your visibility on the network. You can toggle your "Available to Donate" status at any time, which immediately ceases any location tracking or notification processing.
