Our Story

Blood connects us.
Technology unites us.

Saarthi was born from a simple belief — that in a medical emergency, the nearest willing donor should find the nearest patient, instantly.

Our Mission

India loses thousands of lives every year because the right blood doesn't reach the right person in time. Not because donors don't exist — but because the connection doesn't happen fast enough.

Saarthi changes that. We use hyperlocal matching and AI-driven donor ranking to bridge this gap — ensuring that when someone needs blood, help is never more than a few minutes away.

The name "Saarthi" means companion or guide in Hindi — because in your most difficult moment, we are there to guide help to your door.

Technology

How it works under the hood

Built with thoughtful engineering to make every second count in an emergency.

🤖
KNN ML Model
Our K-Nearest Neighbours model ranks donors by proximity, availability, past donation history, and response rate — selecting the most likely-to-respond donors first.
⚡
Real-time Matching
Requests are processed in milliseconds. The moment you post, our backend identifies and notifies the top matched donors simultaneously via the dashboard.
📍
Hyperlocal Focus
We prioritise donors within the same city and neighbourhood. Closer donors arrive faster — a feature often overlooked in national-level platforms.
🔐
OTP Verification
All donor accounts are verified via email OTP using Brevo SMTP. Fake or inactive accounts are automatically filtered from the matching pool.
🌐
Google OAuth
One-click sign-in via Google makes registration frictionless. Your Google account identity provides an additional layer of trust and verification.
📊
Donor Scoring
Each donor accumulates a response rate score. Donors who consistently accept requests are ranked higher in future matches, improving overall platform reliability.
