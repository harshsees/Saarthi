Return Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Saarthi is a non-profit donor connection platform. We do not sell products or services, and therefore do not have a standard commercial return policy.

Donations

Blood donations are voluntary and cannot be "returned" once the procedure is complete. If you have concerns about a donation experience, please contact the hospital where the donation took place.

Monetary Contributions

If you have made a voluntary contribution to support our technology costs and would like to request a refund, please contact us within 7 days of the transaction.
