Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

At Saarthi, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to connect donors with patients effectively:

  • Account Info: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Medical Info: Blood group and donation history.
  • Location: City and real-time geographic coordinates (only when active).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of matching donors with emergency blood requests. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your sensitive information. Access to your contact details is only provided to matched donors/patients after mutual consent.
